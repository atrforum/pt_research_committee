About

Established in 2023, the Australasian Transport Research Forum - Public Transport Research Committee (ATRF-PTRC) is a team of transportation professionals from academia, government and industry volunteering to push public transport research stronger in Australasia.

The aim of ATRF-PTRC is:

“to promote, improve and enhance quality public transport research in Australia / New Zealand and to better focus research on the needs of Australasian public transport (applied research).”

We operate as a standing committee of the Australasian Transport Research Forum Inc. Our major activities are :
  • to document RESEARCH NEEDS in the field
  • to run topical WEBINARS in topical areas of public transport research
  • to support the work of ATRF in its ANNUAL CONFERENCE notably in generating research contributions in the area of public transport
  • to encourage funding of public transport research focussed on industry needs.

The committee meets every few months to further these activities. You can become a friend of the committee by contacting the committee chair. All members and friends of the committee must be members of ATRF Inc. See details on how to apply for membership here.
